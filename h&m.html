H & M

“Fashion and quality at the best price in a sustainable way.” Offering fashion and quality at the best price, H&M offers a varied assortment for the entire family, including concepts for ladies, men, teenagers and children. A family of unique brands united by common passions: to make great design accessible to everybody and to lead the way towards a more inclusive and sustainable world.Founded in 1947, H&M is a global fashion brand and pioneer of design collaborations with style icons such as Karl Lagerfeld, Stella McCartney, Viktor & Rolf, Roberto Cavalli, Comme des Garçons, Sonia Rykiel and Lanvin.
